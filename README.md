# Ask-Me : Assignment-11-Category-9

**_Ask me_**  is a product recommendation platform,
where users can post their Query about a product and other can suggest him/her according to experience or knowledge which helps users to make the best decision to buy a product or not.

##Features of Ask Me :
* Post queries about any gadget and get opinions from others
* Recommend solutions and share opinions on others' queries
* View all recently posted queries
* See recommendations you have posted on others' queries
* Track all your posted queries and responses from others

## Explore "Ask me" : 

If you want to use Ask me efficiently you have to Signup first then you can post your query about any gadget and have people's opinion about you posted query. You can also recommend others on thei query and share you opninion.

on users side you will see all recently posted queries , your recommendations that you posted on others query , all of your posted queries and other recommendations on your asked query
